1. Which statement best expresses the mole concept?
A. One mole is the mass of one molecule expressed in grams
B. One mole contains exactly 6.022 × 10²³ specified entities

D. One mole is numerically equal to the atomic number
Answer: B  |  Type: Single Correct MCQ  |  Level: NEET  |  NCERT topic: Mole and Avogadro constant
Explanation: A mole is an amount of substance containing Avogadro’s number of specified entities.
