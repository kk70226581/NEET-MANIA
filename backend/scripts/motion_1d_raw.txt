1. A particle moves 30 m east and then 20 m west in 10 s. Its average speed and magnitude of average velocity are:

Explanation: Total distance is 50 m, displacement is 10 m east; divide each by 10 s.

2. A body covers the first half of a distance with speed 20 m/s and the second half with speed 30 m/s. Its average speed is:

C. 26 m/s
D. 10 m/s
Answer: A  |  Type: Single Correct MCQ  |  Level: NEET  |  Topic: Average speed
Explanation: For equal distances, average speed is 2v₁v₂/(v₁+v₂)=24 m/s.
